How This One Works

Text To
Steel.

Calligraphy needs one extra step most parts don't — getting the script right before it's ever cut.

01

Share The Text

Send the exact Ayat, name or dua, plus a reference image of the script style you like.

02

Vector Proof

We trace it into a single cuttable line and send you a proof to approve before anything is cut.

03

Cut & Finish

Laser cut to size, deburred, and finished in matte black, raw steel or golden PVD.

Can you cut a script I don't have a clean image of?

Yes — a phone photo of a printed page or even a hand-sketch is enough to start a vector proof from.

Do you do wall-mounted panels, not just clocks?

Yes, any size up to 4×8ft in one piece, larger by joining panels — priced by sq.ft.

What if the proof needs a correction?

Unlimited revisions on the vector proof — nothing goes to the laser until you approve the text.
